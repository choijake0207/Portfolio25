          More About Me

          I first got into development as a junior in college. Despite majoring in business, I was drawn to the creative and complex logical aspects of building web applications. Starting off with resources such as Harvard CS50 and The Odin Project, I started my journey of becoming a developer.

          So far, self teaching has been an incredibly challenging and rewarding journey. Coding was one of those things that I had always assumed I wasn't smart enough to understand. Fast foward to now, I am loving the exploration of all things full-stack. While my earlier focus was on front-end development and UI/UX design, more recently, I have taken a strong interest in system design and cloud architecture.

          In my free time, I love watching movies, weight lifting, jiu jitsu, and trying new foods!
